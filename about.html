About Cup of Joe

Cup of Joe is a prominent Filipino alternative and pop rock band that has rapidly become one of the most exciting acts in the contemporary Original Pilipino Music (OPM) scene. Originating from the cool mountain city of Baguio, the band was formed

in 2018 by a group of high school friends who shared a passion for creating music that speaks to the heart.

The name "Cup of Joe," a common slang for coffee, was initially a spontaneous choice but grew to symbolize their diverse "blends" of musical styles and the comforting, familiar feeling associated with their sound.

The band's musical identity is built on a foundation of relatable, emotionally charged lyrics and a sophisticated blend of acoustic pop, retro, and neo-folk elements. Their songwriting often employs eloquent metaphors and vivid imagery to explore

universal themes of love, hope, friendship, and the complexities of human experience.

This approach, influenced by both OPM legends like the Eraserheads and modern international artists, allows their music to resonate deeply with a broad audience. Lead vocalists Gian Bernardino and Raphaell Ridao share the responsibility of

delivering these narratives, backed by the cohesive instrumentation of Gabriel Fernandez (lead guitar), CJ Fernandez (rhythm guitar), and Xen Gareza (keyboards).

Gian Bernardino

Vocals

Gian Bernardino is the vocalist and guitarist for the popular Filipino OPM band Cup of Joe, He is a singer-songwriter from Villasis, Pangasinan, with a strong musical background, as his parents were orchestra performers. He is recognized for his authentic and unconventional approach to being a rock star and for his work in the band, which has achieved significant success.

Raphaell Ridao

Vocals

Raphaell Ridao is a Filipino singer-songwriter, composer, and a lead vocalist for the popular OPM (Original Pilipino Music) band Cup of Joe. He is known for co-writing and performing hit songs such as "Multo", "Tingin" (with Janine Teñoso), and "Mananatili".
